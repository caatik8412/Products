Site & Inventory Ledger — For Infrastructure Contractors
Contractors handling government or private infrastructure projects deal with material worth crores across multiple sites simultaneously. This system gives you a single live ledger — every item issued, consumed, and remaining at each site, matched against the project scope. Your project managers know exactly what's on ground, your accounts team knows what's been billed, and nothing falls through the cracks between the two.
Multi-site tracking Material consumption vs scope Real-time stock visibility Budget utilisation
🛒
Micro POS & Billing Tracker — For Retail & Trading Businesses
Built for shops and trading businesses that move high volumes of items daily and need their billing, stock, and cash flow tracked without complexity. Every sale updates your stock automatically, every payment method is recorded, and your day-end summary is ready without manual counting. GST is calculated and applied at the point of sale — no separate entry, no mistakes, no delays.
Instant GST billing Live stock deduction Cash / UPI / Card split Daily sales summary
💼
Office Management Workspace — For Consultancy Firms
Consultancy firms run on client engagements, not tasks — and managing ten simultaneous projects across a team is where things go wrong quietly. This workspace gives you a live view of every engagement: what stage it's at, who owns it, what's overdue, and how loaded each team member actually is. Your principals focus on delivery while the system handles visibility — no chasing, no status meetings, no surprises at invoicing time.
Engagement pipeline board Consultant utilisation tracking Deadline & escalation flags Client delivery status
📋
Tax Compliance OS — For CA & Tax Practices
Managing compliance for multiple clients means holding dozens of overlapping deadlines in your head simultaneously — GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, TDS returns, advance tax, ITR filings, all on different cycles for different clients. This system replaces that mental load with a structured, month-by-month compliance calendar for your entire client base. One glance tells you what's filed, what's pending, and what's at risk — so nothing slips and no penalty lands on a client's desk.
GST & Income Tax calendar Multi-client deadline view Filing status tracking Overdue alerts
